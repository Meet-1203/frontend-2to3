What is websites ?
Websites are typically dedicated to a particular topic or purpose,such as news, education, commerce, entertainment, or social media .
Hyperlinking between web pages guides the navigation of the site, which often starts with a home page.
The most visited sites are GOOGLE,YOUTUBE and FACEBOOK.
All publicy-accessible websites collectively constitute the WORLD WIDE WEB.
User can access websites on a range of devices, including desktops, laptops, tablets and smartphones.
