What is URL?
Full form :Uniform Resource Locator.
It is a refrence (an address)to a resource on the internet.
URL consist of multiple parts--including a protocol and domain name--
   that tell web browsers how and where to retrive a resource.
HTTP or HTTPS.It's a network communication protocol connecting web servers and web browsers.
www.Also called a subdomain, it's the part before a URL's first dot.
URL's aree important for two ways main reasons: firstly,because they help us organise and find things on the internet;secondly,because they define the URL structure of a website.
Having a good URL structure is important to make sure users can easily navigate your site and for ranking well in search engines.
